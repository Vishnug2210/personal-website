About me

I am a young enthusiastic artist from Kerala in Inda. I started sketching as a hobby but gradually it becomes a profession. I am mainly focusing on portrait sketching and landscape paintings. No matter the medium, I want my art to inspire people and realize them the beauty of mother nature

I completed my degree in TKM College of Engineering Kollam. After college, I started painting as a medium for stress relief and to find inner peace. I will post all my works here and you can contact me through this website.
